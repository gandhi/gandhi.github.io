Collider Augmentation (02/16)
Making UMD's hackerspace fully accessible. 
When Terrapin Hackers was founded, the members needed a place to work. Enter Collider. Collider is a 1000 sq. ft. room in the basement of the chemistry building at the University of Maryland. The space is filled with a ton of hardware and tools to fulfill any hacker's needs, but the only people who could get in were those with swipe access. <a href = "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rq76U-nN76M">We changed that</a>.
